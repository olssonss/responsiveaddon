Why Vancouver?

Vancouver

With its scenic views, mild climate, and friendly people, Vancouver is known around the world as both a popular tourist attraction and one of the best places to live. Vancouver has hosted many international conferences and events, including the 2010 Winter Olympics and 2010 Winter Paralympics.

My Favorite Hiking spots

Stanley Park Seawall

The beautiful area now known as Stanley Park was once home to many Indigenous peoples and remains a culturally significant area for the local First Nations people today.

False Creek Loop

False Creek Loop has fantastic views of downtown Vancouver. The route is perfect for walking, biking or running. There are plenty of sitting areas and benches for taking a break, as well as scenic areas for taking photos.

Difficulty Distance Duration
Medium 6,4 miles Ca 2h
Learn More

Queen Elizabeth Park

Queen Elizabeth Park's peaceful gardens are great place to spend an afternoon. In the middle of the city and easily accessible (and free), the park offers paved paths, hills, ponds, and lots of flowers.

Difficulty Distance Duration
Easy 1,4 miles Ca 30 min
